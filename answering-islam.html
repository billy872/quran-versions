Answering Islam

A growing compendium of careful, source-cited responses to common Muslim apologetic questions. Truth-focused, gentle in tone, and rooted in the texts both traditions actually share.

Our Approach

This page is a living index. Each entry begins with a claim that is commonly raised, then offers an answer that prefers what the texts actually say over what either side wishes they said. We cite chapter and verse. We do not paraphrase to win. When we do not know, we say so.
